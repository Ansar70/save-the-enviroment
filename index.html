About Environmental Protection

Environmental protection means conserving our natural resources like forests, oceans, air, and wildlife. Human activities have increased pollution levels, making it essential for us to take responsible actions to protect Earth.

Major Causes of Pollution

  • Industrial smoke and chemical waste
  • Vehicle emissions
  • Deforestation
  • Plastic usage
  • Burning fossil fuels

Effects on Our Planet

  1. Global warming
  2. Climate change
  3. Rising sea levels
  4. Health problems
  5. Loss of biodiversity

Types of Pollution

Type Causes Impact
Air Smoke, Vehicles Respiratory issues
Water Industrial waste Water diseases
Land Plastic & dumping Soil damage

Solutions: The 3 R’s

Reduce
Minimize waste and conserve energy.
Reuse
Use items multiple times instead of discarding them.
Recycle
Process materials to make new products.
